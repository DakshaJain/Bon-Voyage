ABOUT US

It's all about travel



Nurtured from the seed of a single great idea - to empower the traveller - Bon Voyage is a pioneer in India's online travel industry. Founded in the year 2020, Bon Voyage came to life to empower the Indian traveller with instant bookings and comprehensive choices. The company initiated its journey serving the US-India travel market offering a range of best-value products and services powered by technology and round-the-clock customer support.

After consolidating its position in the market as a brand recognised for its reliability and transparency, Bon Voyage launched its India operations in 2021. With more and more Indians initiating to transact online with IRCTC and new opportunities with the advent of low cost carriers, Bon Voyage offered travellers the convenience of booking travel online with a few clicks.

Bon Voyage's rise has been led by the vision and the spirit of each one of its employees, for whom no idea was too big and no problem too difficult. With untiring determination, Bon Voyage has proactively diversified its product offering, adding a variety of online and offline products and services.
